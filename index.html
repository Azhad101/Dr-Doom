Welcome to Latveria

EXPLORE THE WORLD OF DOOM BATTLEWORLD

Battleworld is a patchwork planet created by Doctor Doom.

Doom stole Beyonder powers in the Secret Wars storyline to create it. It serves as a solitary, curated remnant of the Marvel Multiverse following its destruction by incursions.

Dr Doom's Feats

The greatest Marvel villain, Doom's genius is paired with a fierce ego, allowing him to challenge gods and rewrite reality itself.
